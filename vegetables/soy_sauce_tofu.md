# Soy sauce tofu

Lazy

Serves:

Preparation time:

Cooking time:

## References

[Adam Liaw](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JH9rHe6-Xyc)

## Ingredients

- 1 block silken tofu
- 1 1/2 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 4 cloves chopped garlic
- 1/2 diced onion
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 sliced spring onion

## Directions

1. Turn tofu upside down with paper towl under to absorb more liquid
2. Heat pan on low-medium and add oil, garlic, and onion
3. Remove from heat and add soy sauce
4. Add on tofu with spring onion
