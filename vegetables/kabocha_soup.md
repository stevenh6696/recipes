# Kabocha soup

Dad made this too

Serves:

Preparation time: 30+ minutes

Cooking time: ~15 minutes

## References

Mom and dad

## Ingredients

- 1 kabocha squash
- 1 onion
- Water
- Milk
- Salt
- Pepper
- Italian seasoning

## Directions

1. Steam halved squash and onion
2. Remove squash skin
3. Blend together + a little water
4. Add milk and cook
5. Add seasoning
