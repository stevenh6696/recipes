# Red yeast pork

炸紅糟肉

Serves:

Preparation time: 4+ hours

Cooking time:

## References

[黃聖凱]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=47M6nOZBhO4)

## Ingredients

- Marinade
  - 1 part 紅糟醬
  - 1 part rice wine
- Pork belly
- 地瓜粉

## Directions

1. Marinade pork belly for at least 3 hours
2. Cover in 地瓜粉 and let sit for a bit
3. Fry for a bit

## Extra notes

- Need to let marinade longer
- Sauce can be ketchup + 甜辣醬
