### Tart Shell
- 1.5 cups flour
- 2/3 cup confectioner's sugar
- 5 1/4 tbsp cornstarch

### Custard Pudding
- 5 sheets of gelatin
- 3 egg yolks
- 45 g sugar
- 250 g milk
- 100 g heavy cream
- 20 g condensed milk
- red tea (original uses vanilla bean)

### Caramel Sauce
- 50 g sugar (4 tbsp)
- pinch of salt
- 10 g water (2/3 tbsp)
- 20 g hot water

Alternatively (from Carla's Creme Caramel)
