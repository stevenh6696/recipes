# Fry roast chicken

Short notes

Serves:

Preparation time:

Cooking time:

## References

[Chinese cooking demystified](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z_LAJKezDOs)

## Ingredients

- 600 grams bone in chicken leg
- Marinade
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  - 1/2 teaspoon chicken bouillon powder
  - 1/8 teaspoon sugar
  - 1/8 teaspoon MSG
  - 1/4 teaspoon white pepper powder
  - 1 teaspoon corn starch
  - 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  - 1 teaspoon chili oil
  - 1 tablespoon peanut oil
- Vegetables
  - Red bell pepper
  - Green bell pepper
  - 8-10 chunks white onion
  - 5 scallion whites
  - 1 inch ginger (sliced)
  - 1 clove garlic (sliced)
- Seasoning
  - 1/4 teaspoon salt
  - 1/8 teaspoon black pepper

## Directions

1. Cut chicken into 1 1/2 inch chunks
2. Marinade chicken and leave for 15 minutes
3. Heat pan until water sizzles away
4. Add chicken in and spread out
5. Center and tilt to each side, each 20 seconds, total 1 1/2 minutes (for brown chicken)
6. Cover for 2 - 3 minutes
7. Flip chicken pieces
8. Repeat step 5
9. Add garlic and ginger
10. Repeat step 5
11. Add peppers, onion, and scallion
12. Fry for 30 seconds more
13. Add seasoning
14. Fry for 30 seconds more

## Extra notes

- Can try other meats too (pork, fish, frog)
